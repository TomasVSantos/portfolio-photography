At the Town Hall, the celebration brought the club and the town into the same frame. The team gathered along the balcony while people watched from below, with a Torreense flag hanging over the railing. The red and blue light changed the familiar civic building into a public stage for the cup's return to Torres Vedras. I photographed the balcony because it showed the victory being received by the wider community, not only by those who had travelled to Jamor.
