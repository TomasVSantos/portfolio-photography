Before kickoff, the large Torreense flag moved across the crowd and took over most of the view, hiding many of the people holding it. At that point there was no result to celebrate, only the expectation that had brought everyone to Jamor. I kept the obstruction in the photograph because it showed how supporters were making the space their own before the match began. The flag carried their belief visibly, while the faces beneath it became part of a collective presence rather than individual portraits.
