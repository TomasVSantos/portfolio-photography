I noticed the framed “Linkinata” sign while walking around Lisbon before the festival. The combination of Linkin Park and Pastéis de Belém was simple enough to understand without explanation, and it placed an international festival act inside a very local reference. I photographed it as one of the small details that became part of attending Rock in Rio, even away from the stages. It made the festival feel present in the city before the music itself became the focus.
